Source: Reddit r/college - Move-in inspection tips
Always inspect the apartment before signing. Look for mold under sinks and in bathrooms. Check all appliances work. Read your entire lease before signing. Hidden fees like carpet cleaning can cost hundreds. Find out exactly which utilities are included. Many listings say utilities included but only cover water. Document the condition of the apartment on move-in day. Take photos and videos of every room. Email them to your landlord the same day so there is a timestamp. Check cell phone signal inside the unit before signing. Ask neighbors how responsive the landlord is for repairs. Walk the neighborhood at night before committing. Never pay a deposit in cash. Always pay by check or bank transfer.
