Source: Reddit r/Apartmentliving - Landlord problems and tenant rights
In most states landlords are legally required to provide working heat within 24 to 72 hours of a repair request during winter. Always submit repair requests in writing via email not just calling or texting. Email creates a documented paper trail. If landlord does not respond send a formal written notice. Most cities have a tenant rights hotline for free legal advice. Contact city housing code enforcement if landlord ignores repair requests. Know your security deposit rights. Landlord must return deposit within 14 to 30 days of move-out. Normal wear and tear cannot be charged against your deposit.
