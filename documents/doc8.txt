Source: Reddit r/Apartmentliving - Safety tips for first-time renters
When you move in ask landlord to rekey the locks as you do not know who has copies of old keys. Check that your door has a deadbolt. Test every smoke detector on your first day and replace batteries if needed. Locate the fire extinguisher in your unit on day one. Never leave cooking food unattended on the stove. Package theft is extremely common in apartment complexes. Use Amazon Hub lockers when possible. Pause all deliveries before going home for break. Never wire money or send gift cards as a security deposit. Legitimate landlords accept checks or official bank transfers. Always verify the landlord owns the property before paying anything.
