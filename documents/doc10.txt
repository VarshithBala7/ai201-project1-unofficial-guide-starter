Source: Reddit r/college - Dorms vs off-campus honest comparison
Dorm room with required meal plan costs 13200 dollars per year. Off campus 2 bedroom apartment split with roommate costs 8400 dollars per year. Annual savings living off campus approximately 4800 dollars. Hidden costs of off-campus include transportation 600 to 900 dollars per year, furniture 500 to 1500 dollars upfront, time cost of cooking and cleaning adding 7 to 10 extra hours per week. Advantages of off-campus include more space and privacy, freedom to cook own food, can have pets, no resident advisor, builds real life skills. Disadvantages include no dining hall when tired, farther from campus, all problems are your responsibility, less social interaction, easier to feel isolated.
