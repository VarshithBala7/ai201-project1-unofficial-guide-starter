Source: Reddit r/college - Questions to ask before signing a lease
Ask how old the building is and when appliances were last replaced. Ask what the heating source is gas or electric as this affects utility costs. Ask if laundry is in unit or shared. Ask if there are any known pest problems. Ask what the penalty is for breaking the lease early. Ask if subletting is allowed. Ask how much written notice is required before moving out. Ask what the security deposit covers. Ask who to contact for repairs and what the typical response time is. Ask if there is an emergency maintenance line for nights and weekends. Ask to speak with a current tenant about their experience.
