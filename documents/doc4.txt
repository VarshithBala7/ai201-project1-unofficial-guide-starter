When both people are on a lease it is called joint and several liability. The landlord can hold either tenant responsible for full rent. If your roommate does not pay you are still legally responsible. You can pay full rent to avoid eviction then sue roommate in small claims court. Always create a written roommate agreement before moving in. Include how rent is split and when it is due. Include cleaning responsibilities with specific schedule. Include quiet hours for the apartment. Include guest policies for overnight guests. Include what happens if someone wants to move out before lease ends.
