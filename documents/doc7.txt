Source: Reddit r/college - When and how to search for apartments
For fall semester move-in start searching in January or February which is 7 to 8 months before move-in. Best selection is available in February and March. Most desirable units are already signed by April. Last minute search from May through August means limited options. For spring semester start searching in September and October. Search on Zillow and Apartments.com. Check Facebook groups for your university for housing posts. Ask your university off-campus housing office for landlord lists. Walk neighborhoods near campus as many landlords only post signs in windows. Ask grad students which landlords are good and which to avoid.
