Source: Reddit r/Apartmentliving - How to get your full security deposit back
Before unloading any boxes do a full walkthrough of the empty apartment. Photograph every single room from multiple angles. Document every scratch scuff stain and broken item. Record a video walkthrough narrating what you see. Email all photos and video to your landlord the same day. At move-out clean the apartment thoroughly. Clean inside the oven completely. Scrub bathrooms including grout lines. Patch all nail holes with spackle. Request the landlord do a joint walkthrough before handing in keys. Get a written receipt when you return the keys. Most states require landlords to return deposits within 14 to 30 days.
