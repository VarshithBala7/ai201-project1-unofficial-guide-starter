Source: Reddit r/Apartmentliving - Maintenance and repair guide
Things you can fix yourself include clogged drains using a drain snake, running toilet by replacing the flapper valve for five dollars, squeaky door hinges with WD-40, small nail holes filled with spackle, tripped circuit breakers by switching off then on, smoke detector beeping from low battery needing a 9V replacement. Always report to landlord in writing any water leak or water damage because unreported leaks cause mold. Report any pest sightings as pest control is the landlords legal responsibility. Report broken locks immediately. Submit repair requests in writing via email with your name unit number date and description. Follow up in writing if no response within 48 hours.
